The Impossible Triangle

Engineers face three competing demands every day: product performance, manufacturing cost, and sustainability targets. Today, they navigate this blind — cost data is scattered across 5-10 systems, CO2 data arrives months after decisions are made, and compliance requirements are checked at the end, not the beginning.

The result: expensive late-stage rework, missed sustainability targets, and design decisions made on gut feeling instead of evidence.

Design-to-Profit Intelligence

TriForge is an intelligence layer for CAD and PLM workflows that connects manufacturing cost, material-level carbon data, and compliance requirements into a single trade-off view — at the point where engineers can still act on it.

Cost Transparency

See the manufacturing cost impact of every material and process choice, connected to real data, not generic estimates.

Carbon Visibility

Understand the CO2 consequences of design decisions in real time, not months later in a separate LCA report.

Compliance by Design

Surface regulatory requirements (ESPR, DPP, substance compliance) as engineering constraints, not afterthoughts.
