5. Your Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")

  • Data portability – receive your data in a structured format

  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact: contact@loopforgelab.com

6. Do We Share Your Data?

No. We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Email communications are stored locally within our team and not processed by external services.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data (encryption, secure email storage, access controls). However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
